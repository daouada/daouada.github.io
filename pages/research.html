Research

Our research focuses on advancing the fields of Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Machine Learning, with particular emphasis on 3D Shape Modeling, Multi-modal Sensing, and Geometric Deep Learning. We develop innovative solutions for real-world applications including Face Recognition, DeepFake Detection, Human Body Modeling, and Space Situational Awareness.

Research Areas

3D Shape Modeling

Research on geometric modeling and analysis of 3D shapes, focusing on:

  • Geometric modeling of rigid and non-rigid 3D shapes
  • Statistical and topological modeling
  • Deep learning approaches for 3D reconstruction
  • Human body modeling and reconstruction
Related Publications →

DeepFake Detection

Cutting-edge research in digital media manipulation detection:

  • Multi-modal deepfake detection
  • Temporal analysis of manipulated videos
  • Face forgery detection
  • Robust detection methods
Related Publications →

Space Situational Awareness

Advanced research in space object detection and tracking:

  • Spacecraft pose estimation
  • Space object tracking
  • Multi-sensor fusion for space applications
  • Deep learning for space imagery
